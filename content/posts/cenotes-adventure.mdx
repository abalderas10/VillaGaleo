Dive into the magical world of cenotes, natural sinkholes filled with crystal-clear freshwater that were sacred to the ancient Maya civilization.

## What are Cenotes?

These natural wonders are formed when limestone bedrock collapses, revealing groundwater pools:
- Natural swimming pools
- Underground cave systems
- Crystal clear waters
- Rich history and mythology

## Best Cenotes Near Cancún

### Cenote Dos Ojos
- Two connected sinkholes
- Perfect for snorkeling
- Cave diving opportunities
- Clear visibility

### Gran Cenote
- Open-air cenote
- Family-friendly
- Turtle spotting
- Stunning rock formations

### Cenote Azul
- Open cenote
- Perfect for swimming
- Cliff jumping spots
- Fish watching

## Planning Your Visit

### What to Bring
- Biodegradable sunscreen
- Water shoes
- Snorkel gear
- Waterproof camera

### Best Time to Visit
- Early morning for fewer crowds
- Midday for best lighting
- Year-round activity
- Avoid rainy season
