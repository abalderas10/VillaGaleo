Welcome to your ultimate guide to snorkeling in Cancún! As a local marine enthusiast and experienced guide, I'm excited to share some of the most breathtaking spots that often get overlooked by typical tourist routes.

## Best Hidden Spots

### Punta Nizuc
Located at the southern tip of Cancún's Hotel Zone, this protected area offers:
- Shallow reefs perfect for beginners
- Abundant sea life including sea turtles
- Crystal clear waters with 20m visibility

### Puerto Morelos National Reef Park
Just 20 minutes from VillaGaleon:
- Part of the Mesoamerican Barrier Reef
- Home to over 65 species of coral
- Perfect for spotting eagle rays

## Tips for the Perfect Experience

1. **Best Time to Go**
   - Early morning (7-9 AM) for calmest waters
   - November to April for best visibility

2. **What to Bring**
   - Reef-safe sunscreen
   - Underwater camera
   - Light snack and water

3. **Conservation Notes**
   - Never touch the coral
   - Maintain a safe distance from marine life
   - Follow guide instructions
