Just a short boat ride from Cancún lies the magical Isla Mujeres, a paradise island that combines natural beauty with rich history and culture.

## Getting There

The journey is part of the adventure! From VillaGaleon, you have two options:
- Private boat tour with La Donostiarra
- Ferry service from Puerto Juárez

## Must-Visit Spots

### Playa Norte
Consistently ranked among the world's best beaches:
- Crystal clear waters
- Powder-soft white sand
- Shallow swimming areas
- Perfect sunset views

### Punta Sur
The island's highest point offers:
- Ancient Maya temple ruins
- Spectacular cliff views
- Sculpture garden
- Sunrise viewing point

### MUSA Underwater Museum
A unique snorkeling experience:
- Over 500 submerged sculptures
- Artificial reef system
- Clear visibility
- Suitable for all skill levels

## Local Experiences

### Food & Dining
- Fresh seafood at local restaurants
- Traditional Yucatecan cuisine
- Beachfront dining options
- Local market visits

### Activities
1. Snorkeling with whale sharks (seasonal)
2. Golf cart island tours
3. Sea turtle sanctuary visits
4. Local artisan workshops

## Planning Your Visit

### Best Time to Visit
- December to April: Perfect weather
- May to September: Whale shark season
- July to August: Peak season

### Tips
- Book activities in advance
- Bring reef-safe sunscreen
- Carry cash for local vendors
- Start early to avoid crowds
