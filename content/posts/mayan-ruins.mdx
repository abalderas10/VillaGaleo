The Riviera Maya holds some of the most impressive Maya archaeological sites in the world. Let's explore these ancient wonders that are easily accessible from VillaGaleon.

## El Rey Archaeological Zone

Right in Cancún's Hotel Zone:
- Walking distance from VillaGaleon
- 47 ancient structures
- Resident iguanas
- Perfect for quick visits

## Tulum Ruins

The only Maya city built on the coast:
- Dramatic cliff-top location
- Well-preserved structures
- Beautiful beach access
- Stunning photo opportunities

## Chichén Itzá

UNESCO World Heritage site:
- The famous El Castillo pyramid
- The largest ball court in Mesoamerica
- Sacred cenote
- Impressive acoustics

## Planning Your Visit

### Best Times to Visit
- Early morning or late afternoon
- Less crowded on weekdays
- Avoid peak heat hours

### What to Bring
- Comfortable walking shoes
- Water bottle
- Hat and sunscreen
- Camera

### Transportation Options
- Private tour from VillaGaleon
- Rental car
- Organized group tours
